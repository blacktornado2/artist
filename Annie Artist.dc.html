About the Artist

Anisha Goyal

Painter · Business Analyst

By day, I work as a business analyst — finding meaning in numbers, translating complexity into clarity. It is precise, logical, and satisfying. But it is not where I find beauty.

Painting is where I find beauty. My landscapes and still lifes begin with long, unhurried looking — at the way evening light lands on a hillside, or the quiet presence of fruit on a table. Each canvas is a record of that attention.

I work primarily in oil on canvas and linen. My subjects are landscapes and still lifes — ordinary moments made worth pausing for.
